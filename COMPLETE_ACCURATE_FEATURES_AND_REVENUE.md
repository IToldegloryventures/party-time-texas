- **NFC Scans**: Charges based on the volume of NFC scans processed beyond a plan's included limit
- **API Calls**: Charges for extensive use of platform APIs for integrations
- **Storage**: Fees for storing large volumes of data (e.g., high-resolution event photos, extensive analytics logs)
- **Bandwidth**: Charges for high data transfer volumes, especially for high-traffic landing pages or event media
